How to Play UNO

This is a single-player game against an AI opponent. The goal is to be the first to play all your cards.

Game Flow

On your turn, you must match the top card of the discard pile by color or number/symbol.

  • To **play a card**, click on a card in your hand. If it's a valid move, it will be played.
  • To **draw a card**, click the card deck in the center.
  • You can always play a **Wild** card or a **Wild Draw Four** card, regardless of the discard pile.
  • When you have only **one card left**, click the "UNO!" button to call out "UNO!".

Action Cards

These cards have special effects when played:

  • Skip: Skips the next player's turn.
  • Reverse: Reverses the direction of play. (Acts as a Skip in this 2-player game)
  • Draw Two (+2): The next player must draw two cards and miss their turn.
  • Wild: Allows the player to change the color of play.
  • Wild Draw Four (+4): Allows the player to change the color and forces the next player to draw four cards and skip their turn.
