Before becoming a legend, Neelama honed his skills and defined his legacy with Kanshiram Nagar FC. It was here that his raw talent and unyielding determination blossomed, setting the stage for his heroic future. He continues to cherish the bonds and lessons learned during his time with the club.

The Unbreakable Spirit

The true cost of victory: Neelama in the hospital after scoring the winning goal.

"In the final moments of the championship, with the score tied, Neelama was faced with a choice: protect his leg or secure victory. Without hesitation, he lunged for the ball, scoring the winning goal at the cost of his own bone. This single act of courage cemented his legacy as a true hero."

- From the official NFA chronicle

This moment defines not just his career, but his character. It is a testament to the fact that for Neelama, nothing is more important than his team's success.

A Heart of Gold

Youth Academy

Neelama established a free football academy for underprivileged children, providing them with training, equipment, and a chance to chase their dreams.

Charity Drives

He regularly organizes and participates in charity drives, raising millions for various causes, from disaster relief to medical research.

Personal Mentorship

Beyond financial support, Neelama dedicates his personal time to mentor young players and community leaders, guiding them with his wisdom and experience.

The President: Johann Vijay

Johann Vijay, the visionary founder and current president of the Neelama Fans Association, is the driving force behind this community. His unwavering passion for football and deep admiration for Neelama's legacy inspired him to create a platform where fans could unite, celebrate, and support their hero's journey.

"Neelama's spirit of sacrifice and generosity is the foundation of everything we do. Our goal is to honor that legacy by building a community that reflects his values."
